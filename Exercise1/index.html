Prep

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

2. Place chicken in a large bowl and season with salt and pepper. Mix in lemon juice, lime juice, and garlic. Mix in onion, and mix well. Pour into a 9x13 inch baking dish. Bake until chicken is no longer pink in the center, 35 to 40 minutes.

3. Remove chicken from oven and let cool for 5 minutes. Serve with lime wedges.
